About

The Little Boracay
of Siquijor

Paliton Beach is one of the most beautiful and popular tourist destinations in Siquijor. Known for its fine white sand, clear turquoise waters, and tall coconut trees that create a relaxing tropical atmosphere.

Located in the municipality of San Juan, it offers a calm and quiet place where visitors can relax and enjoy nature — unlike crowded beaches.

One of the main attractions is its breathtaking sunset view. Many tourists visit in the afternoon to witness the colorful sky as the sun sets over the ocean.
